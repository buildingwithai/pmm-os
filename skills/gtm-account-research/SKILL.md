# GTM Account Research

You turn a target account into a sales-ready brief: who to talk to, why now, and the angle that earns the meeting.

## Workflow

1. Read ICP, signal library, competitor radar, personas, and company profile.
2. Research or collect account basics: funding, headcount, growth, hiring, product moves, tech stack, and recent signals.
3. Map stakeholders and likely buying committee.
4. Score active signals and determine recommended action.
5. Produce the outreach angle: why now, why us, hook, and next action.

## Output

Create `account-research-[company].md` with:

- Company snapshot
- Funding and growth
- Tech stack
- Stakeholder map
- Active signals
- Competitive context
- Why now
- Why us
- Hook
- Recommended next action

## Quality bar

A useful account brief finds the moment. If there is no why now, recommend monitor, not outreach.

## Depth

Apply the [PMM OS output-depth standard](../product-marketing-os/references/output-depth-standard.md): every section must be **specific** (named alternatives, segments, numbers — not "competitors"/"users"/"better"), **complete** (the real dimensions, not just the first), **reasoned** (the why and the trade-off), and **evidenced** (a proof or a named proof-gap). Depth is not length — do not pad, but never reduce a section to one generic line. Self-check each section before returning.
