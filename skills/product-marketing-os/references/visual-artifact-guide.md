# Visual Artifact Guide

Use visual outputs when the deliverable would be stronger as an image, slide, mockup, diagram, or creative concept.

## Good visual outputs

- Product launch hero concept
- Landing page wireframe or section mockup
- Ad creative concept
- Social carousel direction
- Product positioning diagram
- Before-and-after workflow diagram
- Competitive landscape map
- Persona card
- Campaign architecture map
- Launch timeline
- Sales deck outline with slide-by-slide guidance

## When to generate multiple artifacts

For launches and campaigns, default to a pack instead of a single asset:

```text
1 launch brief
1 campaign brief
1 deck outline
1 blog outline or draft
3 email drafts
6 social posts
3 ad concepts
3 image generation briefs
1 sales one-pager
1 battlecard or FAQ
1 measurement plan
```

Use the artifact factory skill to decide which assets are worth producing and which should stay as placeholders.
