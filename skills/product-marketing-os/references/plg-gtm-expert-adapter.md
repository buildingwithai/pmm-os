# PLG GTM Expert Adapter

## Source

Adapted from `Luke2986/plg-gtm-expert`.

The useful pattern is a senior PLG and GTM strategist that works in two modes: structured discovery and strategy or deliverable generation. The framework library covers PLG funnel mapping, AHA moment identification, time to value, growth loops, PQL/PQA, trial selection, churn, expansion, GTM Power System, use case validation, JTBD, segment scoring, strategic narrative, pricing, and metrics.

## Integration into PMM OS

Use `plg-gtm-strategy` when a request is not just a marketing asset but a growth motion question.

Examples:

- Signup is high but activation is weak.
- Trial conversion is weak.
- Sales wants product usage signals.
- The user asks for PLG, PQL, PQA, AARRR, AHA moment, time to value, North Star metric, or growth loops.
- The launch needs an adoption or expansion motion, not just announcement assets.

## Unified flow

```text
Product context
  -> PLG diagnosis
  -> funnel and metric map
  -> growth loop or GTM motion design
  -> experiment roadmap
  -> artifact factory
  -> coach review
  -> analytics and CRM handoff
```

## Default deliverables

- PLG readiness assessment
- PLG funnel map
- AHA moment and time-to-value diagnosis
- Growth loop design
- PQL/PQA model
- Trial or paywall strategy
- North Star and input metric tree
- 30, 60, and 90 day experiment roadmap
- Instrumentation and MCP handoff plan

## Quality bar

Every recommendation must name:

- Segment
- Activation event
- Current metric or missing baseline
- Proposed intervention
- Expected metric movement
- Instrumentation needed
- Owner
- Decision rule
