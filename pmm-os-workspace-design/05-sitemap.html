It's a graph, not a pure tree

A finding lives under its desk, appears in the Findings database, and is reached by Strategy backlinks. A source opens as a side-peek from any citation. The Events desk and Events database share the same records. Primary parent = the desk; databases and backlinks are secondary entry points, deep-linkable as #v-<page>.
